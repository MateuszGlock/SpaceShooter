    Welcome to "The Maverick's Odyssey"!

    Controls:
    - Move your spaceship: Use the mouse to navigate the galaxy. The ship follows the movement of the cursor.
    - Shoot enemies: Click the left mouse button to unleash powerful shots and defend against hostile forces.
    - Pause the adventure: Press the "Escape" key to pause the game and access the pause menu.
    - Explore upgrades: Press "E" to open the upgrades menu, where you can enhance your spaceship's capabilities.

    Objective:
    Embark on a cosmic journey filled with challenges and adversaries. Your mission is to survive and thrive by shooting down enemy ships, collecting gears, and earning points.

    Game Elements:
    - Stars: Watch out for celestial obstacles as you navigate through the galaxy.
    - Enemies: Battle against hostile alien ships to secure your path.
    - Gears: Collect gears to enhance your spaceship's abilities and purchase upgrades.
    - Healthkits: Grab healthkits to replenish your spaceship's health and extend your odyssey.

    Upgrades:
    Visit the upgrades menu to spend your hard-earned gears on improving your spaceship. The best moment for upgrading your ship is between waves, as it may took some time. Enhance your firepower, boost your engines, and upgrade your bullets for a more formidable journey.

    Scoring:
    - Defeat enemies: Each enemy ship you destroy adds points to your score.
    - Collect gears: Accumulate gears to unlock and purchase upgrades in the upgrades menu.

    Health:
    Monitor your spaceship's health indicated at the top of the screen. Avoid enemy attacks to maintain your vitality. The game ends if your health reaches zero.

    The Maverick's Odyssey awaits you! May the stars guide your journey through the cosmos.
